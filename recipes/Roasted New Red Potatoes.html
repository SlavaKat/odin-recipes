Roasted New Red Potatoes

Description

Roasted red potatoes at their best — plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed
with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds small red new potatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt and freshly ground black
    pepper

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and
adjust the oven rack to the lowest position.

Step 2

Toss potatoes with oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Arrange, cut-side down, on a
rimmed cookie sheet or jellyroll pan.

Step 3

Roast potatoes in the preheated oven until tender and golden brown, 20 to 30
minutes. Transfer to a serving dish.

Step 4

Serve and enjoy!
